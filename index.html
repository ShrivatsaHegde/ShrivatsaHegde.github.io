My Projects

Dissertation

March 2023 - July 2023

Identification of cross talk between PTK2/FAK and menopausal status in breast cancer using computational approaches

This project investigated the expression patterns of the PTK2 protein in various breast cancer subtypes and its association with patient outcomes. The study revealed a link between PTK2 expression and the menopausal state of patients with various aspects of breast cancer. The analysis demonstrated that PTK2 expression is associated with higher stemness and proliferation scores in most subtypes, as well as increased Epithelial-to-Mesenchymal transition scores in subtypes of breast cancer, suggesting its possible involvement in tumor progression and metastasis. Done to highligh its potential role in breast cancer

JSS Academy of Higher Education and Research, Mysuru

Summer Internship

November 2022 - February 2023

Identification of Focal Adhesion Kinase as a target for anticancer activity of Terminalia elliptica using computational approaches

This study focused on exploring the therapeutic potential of natural products in breast cancer treatment, specifically utilizing Terminalia elliptica, a traditional plant known for its various medicinal properties. By screening phytobioactives present in Terminalia elliptica using molecular docking, the research identified chebulagic Acid as a promising lead molecule with superior interaction and binding affinity to Focal Adhesion Kinase (FAK) compared to the standard drug podophyllotoxin. These findings highlight the potential of chebulagic Acid could be a viable candidate for breast cancer therapy.

JSS Academy of Higher Education and Research, Mysuru
